About Me

Hey what's up, my legal name is John Battle but I've always gone by Jack. I'm 24, have lived in Minnesota for my entire life, and enjoy collecting vinyl records. If you check out my Discogs page you'll notice I have a massive collection of King Gizzard and the Lizard Wizard records. KGATLW is easily my favorite band but I do enjoy many other bands, believe it or not.

Some of my hobbies include going to live concert events (R.I.P 2020), PC gaming, watching movies, and walking my dog just to name a few.
